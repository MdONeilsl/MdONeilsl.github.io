How to Use

  1. Upload a TTF, OTF, or WOFF font file containing all desired characters. You can find fonts at:
    • Online: Google Fonts, FontSquirrel, or DaFont.
    • On Your Computer: Check C:\Windows\Fonts (Windows) or /Library/Fonts (macOS).
  2. Verify font licensing for your use case.
  3. Enter text. Only characters in the font will be processed; others will be skipped and shown below.
  4. Click "Generate 3D Fonts" to process the text.
  5. Click "Download ZIP" to save the generated glTF (.gltf) files.
