Shape Mixer

Mix shape data from different Second Life avatars and create uploadable XML files

Shape Mixer Interface

Upload your avatar shapes to begin mixing and customizing your appearance.

Instructions

  1. To export a shape: Navigate to Develop > Avatar > Character Tests > Appearance and export to XML format.
  2. To import a shape:
    • From the shape editor window:
    • Locate the "Import" button in the bottom right corner of the "Editing Shape" window.
    • Select the button, choose the saved XML file, and the exported shape will be applied to your character.
