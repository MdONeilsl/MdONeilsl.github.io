Fragmented frame App Tutorial

  • Draw 2D shapes, apply a texture, and export as a 3D mesh (.dae) for Blender.
  • Select a shape from the toolbar, then click and drag on the canvas.
  • Click a shape to select it (highlighted in red).
  • Drag a selected shape to reposition it.
  • Hold S and drag to scale a selected shape.
  • Hold R and drag to rotate a selected shape.
  • Press Ctrl + C to copy a selected shape, Ctrl + V to paste it.
  • Press Delete to remove a selected shape.
  • Upload an image to apply as the canvas texture.
  • Click "Export Mesh" to download the 3D mesh. Save the texture image in the same directory as the .dae file.
